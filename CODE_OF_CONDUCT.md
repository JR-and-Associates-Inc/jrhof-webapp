# Community Code of Conduct

We want repository participation to be welcoming, respectful, historically responsible, and safe.

Participants should be considerate, accept good-faith correction, distinguish documented fact from recollection, respect privacy and intellectual-property rights, and focus criticism on the work. Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, deliberate misinformation, publication of private information, sexualized conduct, or attempts to pressure volunteers are unacceptable.

Maintainers may edit, hide, reject, or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when needed to protect people, data, or the project. Enforcement decisions will consider context, impact, and a participant's response to correction.

Report conduct concerns privately through [the JRHOF contact page](https://jrhof.org/contact/). Do not put sensitive details in a public issue. Security vulnerabilities use the separate private reporting path in `SECURITY.md`.

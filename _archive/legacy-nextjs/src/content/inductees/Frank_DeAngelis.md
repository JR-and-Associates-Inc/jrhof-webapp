Frank DeAngelis was a graduate of Ranum High School in Westminster, Colorado in 1971. He went on educationally to earn a BA from Metro State College in 1978, an MA from the University of Colorado, Denver in 1984 and also a Certificate from the University of Phoenix, Denver in 1994. Today, he and wife Evelyn live in Thornton.

Frank has 17 years of High School coaching experience in the Jefferson County School District. He was a Varsity Assistant for 1 year at Jefferson Senior High, 2 years at Wheat Ridge Senior High and another year at Columbine Senior High School. Frank became the Head Baseball Coach at Columbine Senior High School where he won 3 League Championships and 2 State Championships in his 13 years there. He also had 17 years of Summer League Baseball as Head Coach where he won 10 League Championships and 1 State Championship.

Mr. DeAngelis has accrued many honors and awards, including:

- National Coach of the Year
- Region 7 Baseball Coach of the Year, Colorado High School Coaches Association
- Rocky Mountain News & Denver Post Baseball Coach of the Year for two years
- Jefferson County Board of Control Male Coach of the Year for three years
- Jefferson County Baseball Coach of the Year
- Selected to coach in the Colorado Rockies High School All-State Game
- Selected six times to coach the Jefferson County Baseball All-Star Team in the State All-Star Game
- Mr. Baseball, Colorado High School Baseball Coaches Association
- University of Notre Dame People Who Make a Difference recognition
- Colorado Athletic Directors Association Outstanding School Administrator of the Year
- Colorado Interscholastic Athletic Administrator Award
- Selected to carry the Olympic Torch during the 2002 Winter Olympic Torch Relay
- Jefferson County Association of Educational Office Professionals (JCAEOP) Administrator of the Year

Frank's contributions are many. He was the organizational head for an array of committees and programs:

- President, Colorado High School Baseball Coaches Association
- Director, Columbine and Chatfield Little League Baseball Camps
- Director, All-State Baseball Games
- Colorado State Baseball Committee member
- Chairperson, Colorado High School Activities Association Baseball Committee
- Classification and League Organizing Committee (CLOC) member
- Coordinator, All-State Baseball Games in conjunction with the Colorado Rockies
- School Improvement Committee member
- Regional Representative, American Baseball Congress
- Columbine Building Representative and Scholarship Committee Member, Colorado High School Coaches' Association
